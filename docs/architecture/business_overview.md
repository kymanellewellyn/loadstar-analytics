# **Business Overview**

## **Who We Are**

Loadstar Trucking is an end-dump hauling company that transports bulk materials (dirt, gravel, debris) between job sites, quarries, and landfills for construction and infrastructure.

The business operates a fleet of trucks and drivers to fulfill hauling jobs. Revenue is generated:

* per load capacity  
* per mile driven  
* per contract

Operational costs include:

* fuel  
* labor  
* maintenance  
* downtime

---

## **Business Goal**

Operate a profitable hauling business by maximizing truck productivity while reliably meeting customer delivery commitments.

This breaks down into three core areas:

* **Profitability**: revenue and cost modeling  
* **Productivity**: utilization, downtime, efficiency  
* **Reliability**: SLAs, operational consistency

---

## **Core Objectives**

1. Increase revenue per truck  
2. Control operating costs  
3. Minimize unplanned downtime  
4. Meet customer delivery expectations  
5. Allocate trucks and drivers efficiently  
6. Ensure accurate and timely billing
